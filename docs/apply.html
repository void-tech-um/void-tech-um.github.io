Product

Product Managers spearhead the feature and content strategy for our products. In this cross-functional role, product managers will be responsible for working with designers and developers to conduct user research, assign and prioritize tasks, and ensure the overall successful development of the product.

Development

Developers will work as a team to bring to life a product that solves a real-world problem. They will experience the full product lifecycle, from development to deployment. They will work with both designers and product managers to ensure they are implementing solutions that will be both clean and efficient.

Design

Designers will work together to create the visual aspects of the product. They will ensure that the user experience is both smooth and seamless. They will work with the product managers to decide how a feature should look and how it should work, as well as communicate with the developers to ensure these are correctly implemented.
